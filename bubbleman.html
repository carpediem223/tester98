BUBBLE MAN

Metal Man Quick Man Bubble Man Wood Man Air Man Heat Man Crash Man Flash Man

Tips to Beat the Boss

  • Weapon Weakness: Metal Blade is the best weapon, allowing you to hit him easily, even when he is high in the air.

  • Attack Pattern: Bubble Man shoots 1–3 bubbles that bounce, then jumps over you. Move under him as he jumps to avoid damage.

  • Avoid the Ceiling: Never jump too high; the ceiling is covered in instant-death spikes.

  • Alternative Weapon: If you don't have Metal Blade, the default Buster works, or the Quick Boomerang is effective
  • Water Physics: Use the high-jump ability in the water to maneuver over enemies.

  • Spike Areas: Spike Areas: Be very cautious in the spike-filled, narrow corridors.

  • Jump Strategy: Jump over his blades, not at him, to avoid going getting hit, especially when he tosses three in a row.

  • Frogs: Leave one small frog alive from the large, spawning frogs to prevent them from shooting more at you.
