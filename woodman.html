WOOD MAN

Metal Man Quick Man Bubble Man Wood Man Air Man Heat Man Crash Man Flash Man

Tips to Beat the Boss

  • Atomic Fire(Heat Man's Weapon): This is his biggest weakness. A fully charged shot can take him down in just two hits.

  • Metal Blade (Metal Man's Weapon): Highly effective and fast to fire. Four shots will defeat him.

  • Air Shooter(Air Man's weapon): Good alternative if you don't have Atomic Fire, as it can pierce his shield.

  • Buster Only: Jump over the leaf shield, and fire at him when his shield is down.
  • Watch the Leaves: When Wood Man jumps, the falling leaves will follow you. Simply move back and forth or jump over them as they descend.

  • Shield Timing: Wood Man's main attack is the Leaf Shield. It protects him and flies toward you, requiring you to jump over it

  • Handle Cornering: If he forces you into a corner, try to jump over him to the other side of the room to reset the fight.
