AIR MAN

Metal Man Quick Man Bubble Man Wood Man Air Man Heat Man Crash Man Flash Man

Tips to Beat the Boss

  • Weakness:The LEAF SHIELD (Wood Man) is the primary weakness.

  • Tactics: Get close to Air Man, jump over his tornadoes, and fire the LEAF SHIELD as you approach him.

  • Alternative Weapons: If you run out of Leaf Shield, the Buster works, or you can use the QUICK BOOMERANG from the far left corner.

  • No-Damage Approach: Let tornadoes move slightly, create space, then use the LEAF SHIELD when he is vulnerable after jumping.
  • Air Platforms: Wait for the spikes on the large faces to retract before jumping on them.

  • Lightning Lords: Use the LEAF SHIELD or METAL BLADE to take them out quickly, then ride their clouds to the next platform.

  • Item 1: After acquiring ITEM 1, you can use it to help you navigate tricky jumps across the many pits in the stage.

  • Stage Tips: Be patient as you traverse through this stage. There are several large jumps that require perfect timing. Make sure you have the METAL BLADES to help you take out enemies that are at a diagonal of your position.
