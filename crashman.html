CRASH MAN

Metal Man Quick Man Bubble Man Wood Man Air Man Heat Man Crash Man Flash Man

Tips to Beat the Boss

  • Tip: Be patient with your shots. The fight can be very quick with the right timing.

  • Alternative Weapon: If you are out of Air Shooter ammo, the standard buster is perfectly effective with proper movement.

  • Keep it Moving: Crash Man's stage is vertical based. Enemies will show up to knock you off the ladders. Keep moving and get to the next screen to avoid excessive enemies.

  • Weakness: The Air Shooter is his primary weakness, sending tornadoes upward that often catch him mid-jump.

  • Control His Movements: Crash Man only jumps when you shoot. Use this to force him to jump over you, then shoot again upon landing.

  • Avoid the Bombs: Dont' get caught in the blast radius of the crash bombs he throws after landing.

  • Buster-Only Strategy: If not using the weakness, treat it like a dancing match: wait for him to stand still, fire, jump over him immediately, and repeat.
