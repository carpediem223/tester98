HEAT MAN

Metal Man Quick Man Bubble Man Wood Man Air Man Heat Man Crash Man Flash Man

Tips to Beat the Boss

  • Weakness - Bubble Lead: This weapon is his primary weakness, causing major damage.

  • Fight Pattern: Heat Man stays stationary while firing, then turns into a fire form and charges at you after being hit.

  • Dodge Strategy: When he turns into fire, jump over him and move to the other side of the room to create distance.

  • Alternative Weapons: If low on Bubble Lead, the Quick Boomerang is a decent alternative

  • Weapon Get: You will receive Atomic Fire(chargeable shot) after winning.

  • Vanishing Blocks: In the tricky disappearing block section, try using Item-1 to create your own platforms and skip the hardest jumps.

  • Enemy Strategy: Use the Metal Blade to quickly take out the Fire Tellys and other enemies throughout the level.

  • Hot Dog Enemies: The blue fire-dogs can be taken down with the Metal Blade.
