FLASH MAN

Metal Man Quick Man Bubble Man Wood Man Air Man Heat Man Crash Man Flash Man

Tips to Beat the Boss

  • Weakness: The Metal Blade is highly effective, allowing you to "jiggle" or juggle him. You only need 4 shots (at close range) to win.

  • Alternative Weapons: Crash Bombs work well, especially if placed on the wall near him, as the explosion does significant damage.

  • The "Juggling" Strategy: Stand on the middle or top step, wait for Flash Man to come close, jump over him, and hit him with a Metal Blade.

  • Dodging the Time Stopper: Flash Man freezes you and fires. Jump as he initiates the freeze to change your position, allowing you to avoid his shots.
  • Quick Win: If you are fast enough and use the Metal Blade efficiently, you can destroy him before he even uses his Time Stopper move.

  • Flash Man runs and jumps along the three-step floor layout, attempting to make contact with you.

  • He will stop to use the Time Stopper to freeze you and fire pellets.

  • The best tactic is to stay on the move and keep him in the air or against a wall with consistent shooting.
